Our Services for E-Sports

1. Main Stage

As the venue's epicenter, the pro competition zone prioritizes acoustic design with noise reduction and sound insulation. This shields players from external audio interference while preserving the audience’s immersive viewing experience.

2. Audio System

The E-Sports venue’s audio system requires stereo reinforcement via line arrays (main L/R + rear L/R). To enhance sound reproduction and pickup clarity, ihD customizes speaker/mic configurations using acoustic simulations for vibration/reverberation control.

3. Video System

An excellent E-Sports venue video system requires high-res, low-latency, scalable, redundant, and adaptable display. Matches stream via LED screens in a 3-section layout (home/away/main), with optional rear displays for large venues. The 4K30 transmission features and supports digital-analog mutual backup.

4. Audience Area

As the venue's epicenter, the pro competition zone prioritizes venue capacity scales with positioning: <500 for small/medium sites; thousands for large areas. Seating includes tiered, flat-floor, or VIP options with unobstructed stage/screen sightlines. Audience comfort is enhanced through appropriate seat spacing and optimized acoustics.

5. Broadcast Control Room

The director’s room manages event directing, signal switching, and live broadcast output. It needs to be furnished with switchers, audio control devices, and monitor matrices, which are in line with the relevant competition broadcast design standards.

6. IT Room

A crucial area for storing core servers, network switches, and game recording hard drives, which should support live streaming, competitions, networking, and data transmission.

– Network Infrastructure Solution: Connects competition, commentary & director rooms. Isolates signal transmission/competition networks. Implements on-premises/cloud MAM or micro-data centers.

– Communication Infrastructure Solution: Dedicated player intercoms linking directors, referees, players, coaches & lounges, ensuring stable grouping + isolated audio recording.

7. Commentator Booth

The commentary area requires proximity to the stage with premium acoustic isolation. It accommodates 2-3 commentators, synchronizes live event progress, integrates data input systems, and ensures low-latency signal transmission.

8. Referee & Admin Room

Deploy event management terminals with print/comm gear for competition control, player registration, and penalty resolution.
