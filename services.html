Audio-Visual & multimedia

Audio-Visual consultancy has been at the core of our services since our inception in 2003. With more than 150 years of combined experience, our audio-visual consultants are attentive-to-detail, being meticulous yet thoughtful in their design. We guide contractors throughout the process to deliver the optimal audio-visual results for our clients. We also undertake audio-visual audits to validate the quality assurance for clients.

We work across the full audio-visual portfolios from spatial, technical to operational audio-visual design. By collaborating closely with acoustics and lighting designers, our experts help clients integrate technology into multiple challenging environments. Our experienced consultants save clients CAPEX expenditure and maximising subsequent ROI through better, more flexible and effective design solutions. Through better customer or guest experience, smoother visits or use of facilities, successful presentations etc, the customers or guests are more likely to return.

Specialties of services:

  • Architectural and Area Lighting Control
  • Audio Systems from live-performance, theatre, and auditorium, to mass distributed background music systems
  • Audio-modelling and simulation
  • Audio, Video, and Control Distribution
  • Display Systems for large-format LED and projection-mapping
  • Leisure and Entertainment Sound, Image and Lighting
  • IPTV, Interactive Video, Holographic Display Systems
  • Networked AV and Communications

Through incorporation of USB-extension to meeting and conference room user’s devices, as well as effortless wireless connectivity of the same, we are able to bring about simple, dependable, and better conferencing in all kinds of spaces. BGM Background music systems may appear simple, but we are able to enhance their performance in an unusual way by specially processing the sound in noisy areas so that the normally-unheard low-volume components become audible, making for BETTER sound and a BETTER future for the guests.

For museum and experience centres, we add in an often-forgotten human sense – smell; by using special devices to release professional aromas such as coffee, swamp, spice market etc, we bring increased realism and better experience to the visitors. Use of projection-mapping to create large areas of video, enables transformation of building exteriors, but also of small objects, in fascinating ways, again making for better guest and visitor experience. Attention also to everyday needs, such as our universal need to charge our phones, means that we recommend and add in intelligent POU (power-over-USB) wired- and wireless charging- in all kinds of innovative ways in meeting room, health club, guestroom, and other environments; these devices tend to be more and more IoT-based, being networked and monitored for useful statistics, making for better life for the users, and better data for the hosts. We are working with a manufacturer too, on voice-control using on-premise voice server, to prevent the users voice ever entering the internet, affording better security and privacy.

Information Technology

Our consultants understand the complexities of different IT needs for complete infrastructure-design from fully-wired optical fibre and copper backbones to completely wireless networks. We employ the industry's most accurate wireless LAN design and simulation tools to ensure optimal wireless network performance, security and compliance.

Our knowledge of well-established and cutting-edge technologies allows us to deliver better solutions to meet different clients’ demands as well as the future needs of the facilities. While the users are enjoying the very best of IT services, the systems are functioning at their optimum and with highest reliability. Smoother experience for customers and guests makes for better return rates, as well as better savings through energy-efficient facilities. And enabling IoT devices helps even with staff safety and room-hygiene.

Specialties of services:

  • HSIA High-speed Internet-access
  • IPTV TV and BYOD bring-your-own-device connectivity
  • ELV/Telecom closet design
  • GRMS Guestroom Management system
  • Service Staff Communication system
  • IoT Internet of Things support and incorporation
  • PABX telephone systems and service, on-premise and cloud-based
  • Server room design as well as co-ordination for UPS uninterruptible Power Supply, water-detection, fire-detection and suppression
  • Structured Cabling and Fibre systems
  • Wired & Wireless LAN systems

In addition to designing based upon 802.11ax for use typically by staff, customers and guests, we fulfil the needs of the fast-increasingly-present IoT devices through design of Zigbee, LoRa and Bluetooth wireless networks, making for fully-integrated and powerful IoT-enabled facilities. IoT facilitation allows for example, cleaning staff to wear IoT badges, trackable and with inbuilt HELP button, as well as alerting housekeeping when food trays or trolleys are pushed out into the corridor. We also optimise wireless coverage and cost through simulation of the areas, to avoid over-provision. We deploy efficient people-sensors in our GRMS and Meeting Room systems, enabling hospitality and corporate clients alike to optimise their energy-bills, making for better operational profit.

Security Systems

Our extensive work with ex-armed forces and security chief advisors equip our experts to completely understand the challenging modern networked security systems and the complex infrastructure requirements. Our analytics applications safeguard our designs to provide intelligent and effective surveillance. They also contribute to delivering better defence solutions for building setback and for creating Virtual perimeter fences.

Specialties of services

  • Threat analysis
  • Layer 1/2/3 security
  • Access-Control and Alarm-Monitoring
  • Bomb/explosives/gun-detection
  • Camera Surveillance including customised analytics
  • Biometrics Security
  • Parking Management
  • Enterprise ID Card Issuance System
  • Gate Control/Turnstile
  • Intrusion-Detection
  • Guard Tour enabling

Our business park projects in India are utilising under-car bomb-scanning in conjunction with camera analytics for license-plate recognition to warn of changed layouts from one visit to another, enhancing the security of the 60+ companies based there with better protection against attack. A casino project in southern Philippines is using 2300 cameras, but importantly analytics too, to enhance the staff- and customer-experience. By incorporating virtual tripwire, as well as facial-recognition, persons in forbidden areas, as well as banned persons, are instantly detected.

All of the above examples bring about vastly-increased response and success from the Security systems, in-turn affording our clients Better Security, better collection and also retention of money, and better experience and therefore return rates of customers and guests.
