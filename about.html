AboutUs

Your Trusted Partner for Smart, Integrated Building Solutions

Empowering Asia’s Landmark Projects with Smart, Integrated Solutions

We are a team of passionate consultants and professionals with a strong regional presence across Asia, including Hong Kong, Shanghai, Beijing, Shenzhen, Tokyo, Singapore, Kuala Lumpur, and Bengaluru. Over the years, we have grown into one of the region’s leading consultancy firms, delivering a diverse portfolio of projects specializing in IT, IoT, Security, ELV, Audio-Visual, Acoustics, BMS (Building Management Systems), EPMS (Electrical Power Monitoring Systems), and GRMS (Guest Room Management Systems).

Leveraging our extensive experience, we provide fully coordinated designs and expert project management, earning a reputation for excellence, innovation, and reliability.

Our mission is to transform the ambitions and visions of our clients into reality. We continuously push boundaries with forward-thinking ideas, creating smarter, more efficient solutions for a better future.
